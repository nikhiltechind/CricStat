About

The purpose of the project is to develop an web application that provides the various stats related to IPL. It has following features-
  • Comparison between two players of userâ€™s desire.
  • Stats of individual player.
  • Stats of past matches.
  • Live match score board.
  • Highest Runs getter , most wicket taker etc..
The CricStat-XI application provides 100% accurate and real-time data. The scope of work for this project will cover the development of CricStat-XI Application. This project will be focused on two major features which are collecting and displaying data about Stats of user desire.

Idea behind the Project

There are many application that provides various stats related to IPL and other series , but none have the feature of comparing the stats of players and user being a fan of particular player wants to keep record weather his favorite player is doing better than his competitors. So in-order to provide the ease of comparison we came up with the idea of Cricstat-XI which along with other stats provides this feature as well. We are also planning to include international matches in the upcoming future.
